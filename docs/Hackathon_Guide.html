🗣️ Prepared Q&A

Q: How does the AI categorization work?
NLP keyword analysis classifying into 6+ categories. Each maps to curated legal roadmaps with IPC sections. Production version would use a fine-tuned LLM on Indian legal corpus.
Q: Why not just use ChatGPT?
ChatGPT hallucinates IPC sections. We provide curated, verified guidance plus RTI generation, lawyer matching, encrypted chat, emergency mode, and 5-layer security.
Q: How do you handle data privacy?
5 layers of security. Encrypted at rest + in transit. Immutable append-only audit logs with REVOKE UPDATE/DELETE. Admin 2FA. Document access triggers push notification to owner.
Q: What's the scalability path?
Vercel edge (auto-scales). Docker Compose → Kubernetes. Redis distributed rate limiting. PostgreSQL → managed RDS.
